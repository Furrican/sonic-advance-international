Upload Date: 10/14/2025

Version 4.4.0 release:
The main options menu has been revamped!

As you can see, the little arrows in the bottom-right corner of most buttons are gone. This is because those buttons act differently. For example, instead of showcasing the menu just to show "ON" or "OFF", pressing the button will simply toggle between "ON" and "OFF", making the option process quicker!

Next, the Soundtrack option is now present in the main options menu and toggling this option makes the options music resets while changing the soundtrack!

There are other informations about this update. Check out the v4.4.0 devlog for more information.
